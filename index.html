Banquet.

Exquisite Venue

Make Your Milestones Unforgettable

Our elegant dining hall offers a versatile backdrop for private celebrations, corporate dinners, or intimate weddings. We provide full-service event support, including customizable dining options, dedicated servers, custom floral decor, and complete table dressing.

The hall features gorgeous natural daylight during the day and warm candlelight ambient lighting during the evening, creating a premium atmosphere that will leave your guests in awe.

Venue Specifications:

  • Capacity: Up to 150 guests seated comfortably
  • Configurations: Round tables, long banquet setups, or cocktail lounge
  • Audio/Visual: Integrated sound system, wireless mic, and HD projection screen
  • Amenities: Private guest entrance, private restrooms, and a dedicated bar
